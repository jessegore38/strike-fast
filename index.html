Frequently Asked Questions

We safely remove all types of snakes found in Central Texas, including rattlesnakes, copperheads, coral snakes, water moccasins, and non-venomous species like rat snakes and garter snakes.

We prioritize emergency calls and typically respond within 30-60 minutes in the New Braunfels area. Response times may vary slightly for surrounding communities.

We follow Texas Parks and Wildlife guidelines for snake relocation. Non-venomous snakes are typically released in appropriate habitats away from residential areas. Venomous snakes are handled according to state regulations.

We offer snake prevention consultations that include identifying and sealing entry points, removing attractants like rodents, and making your property less hospitable to snakes through landscaping recommendations.
